===== DGAC Accra Flight Information Region =====

  * The Accra FIR covers the entirety of [[Ghana]]'s borders as well as [[Benin]]'s and [[Togo]]'s. It is neighbored by [[GOOO|Dakar]] FIR in the West (as well as Dakar Oceanic), the Niamey FIR in the North, the [[DNKK|Kano]] and [[FCCC|Brazzaville]] in the East and [[FNANA|Luanda]] in the South.
